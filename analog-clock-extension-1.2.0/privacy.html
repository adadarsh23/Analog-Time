This Extension does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. Your browsing and usage data remain private and stored only on your device.

The Extension may display weather information from third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies, and we do not collect or store any data through them.

5. Local Storage

All your preferences, alarms, and settings are saved locally in your browser. Uninstalling the Extension will remove this data from your device.
